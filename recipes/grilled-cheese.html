Grilled Cheese

Description

How to make the best lunchtime snack

Ingredients

  • 5 tablespoons of butter, softened and divided
  • 4 slices of sourdough bread
  • 2 cups of shredded cheddar

Steps

  1. Spread 1 tablespoon butter on each slice of bread
  2. In a skillet over medium heat, melt 1 tablespoon butter. Cook until bread is golden and cheese is starting to melt
  3. Repeat for the second sandwich
