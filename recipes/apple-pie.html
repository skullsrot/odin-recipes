Apple Pie

Description

How to make the warmest, 'to die for' apple pie

Ingredients

  • 1 box (14.1 oz) refrigerated Pillsbury Pie Crusts, softened
  • 6 cups thinly sliced, peeled apples
  • 3/4 cup sugar
  • 2 tablespoons all-purpose flour
  • 3/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1/8 teaspoon ground nutmeg

Steps

  1. Heat oven to 425 F. Place 1 pie crust in ungreased 9-ich glass pie plate. Press firmly.
  2. In large bowl, gently mix filling ingredients; spoon into crust-lined pie plate. Top with second crust, press edges together to seal. Cut slits or shapes in several places on top crust.
  3. Bake 40 to 45 minutes or until apples are tender and crust is golden brown. Cover edge of crust with 2 to 3 inch wide strips. Cool on coolig rack for at least 2 hours.
