Cookies

Description

How to make award winning chocolate chip cookies by following these simple and easy steps

Ingredients

  • 4 1/2 cups of all-purpose flour
  • 2 teaspoons baking soda
  • 2 cups of butter
  • 1/2 cup white sugar
  • 4 eggs
  • 2 teaspoons vanilla extract
  • 4 cups of semisweet chocolate chips

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F
  2. Sift flour and baking soda, set aside
  3. Cream together butter and white sugar in a large bowl. Beat until blended. Stir eggs and vanilla
  4. Add flour mix to the butter mixture until well. Add chocolate chips.
  5. Drop cookies onto ungreased cookie sheets
  6. Bake in batches until the edges are golden brown, 10 to 12 minutes
